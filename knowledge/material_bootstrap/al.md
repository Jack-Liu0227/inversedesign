# Aluminum Alloy Design Bootstrap

## Typical Objective Patterns
- Improve UTS while preserving manufacturability and toughness.
- Aging schedule optimization under composition constraints.

## Composition Guidance
- Al is base balance element.
- Zn/Mg/Cu are key strengthening levers; overuse can reduce ductility/corrosion resistance.
- Prefer incremental tuning over broad multi-element perturbation.

## Heat Treatment Method Guidance
- Provide one complete route sentence: solution treatment + quench + aging stages.
- Include clear temperatures and times.

## Candidate Strategy
- Use previous failures to avoid repeated over-aging/under-aging style routes.
- Keep compositions practical for known alloy families.
